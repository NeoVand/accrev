# GLOSSARY — The Accrual World

*Every term the finished story personifies or teaches, with (a) the plain-English
meaning and (b) the character, place, or force that embodies it in the Accrual
World. Completed by the **Assembly agent** from the finished chapters; the canonical
personifications below are the ones the prose actually uses.*

> Note: English-only for this pass. A Farsi column can be added later to align with
