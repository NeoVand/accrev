# Characters — Core Cast (Art Reference)

*Principal-cast portrait references for the Accrual World. One entry per character:
a prose CHARACTER DESCRIPTION (for continuity across every illustration) and a
ready-to-use IMAGE PROMPT for a portrait/reference image.*

**How to use these prompts:** each IMAGE PROMPT describes the **subject only** —
pose, look, props, palette, expression, framing. The global **STYLE SUFFIX** from
`STYLE.md` (rendering style, line quality, lighting language, finish) is appended
to every prompt separately at generation time. Do **not** restate the house style
here; keep these prompts to subject + composition.

**Shared palette (from `src/app.css`).** Keep every character anchored to the
book's world so the cast reads as one family:
- **Warm cream** `#fbf6ee` — the world's paper/air; default background.
- **Soft cream** `#f4ece0` — secondary surfaces, parchment.
- **Dusty terracotta** `#b5635a` — the accent / "recognition" color; the warm
  heartbeat of the realm. Sal's signature.
- **Warm gold** `#a88a5c` — value, ledgers, the Friar, anything sanctified.
- **Dark-plum ink** `#2a1f2d` — linework, text, deep shadow; the "ink" everything
  is ultimately written in.
- Muted plum `#6b5a6e`, faint mauve `#b1a4b1` for mid-tones; sage `#6b8e62`
  (success/credit) and deep red `#a6453f` (danger/debit-pain) used sparingly.

**Casting principle for the transaction-people (Sal, Lyle, Sunny).** A transaction
is an abstract event — a promise of value moving between parties. We personify it
as **a luminous figure made of ledger-light**: a person whose body is sturdy and
human but whose "skin" carries a faint engraving of figures and a soft inner glow,
as if a number learned to walk. The glow's **color encodes recognition status**:
warm terracotta-gold when *recognized* (real, earned, counted), cool and dim when
*deferred/unearned* (owed, waiting). This single rule lets a reader feel a
character's accounting state at a glance, all book long.

## SAL — the Sale (the hero transaction)

**Role.** The protagonist. Summit's first big enterprise deal: the **$90,000
TrailHead Pro bundle sold to Atlas Corp**. A newborn transaction, whole and proud,
$90,000 of pure potential. Eager, naive, enormous-hearted, desperate to *matter* —
to be **recognized**. A *temporary* citizen (a revenue), and so fated to be closed
at year-end: the engine of the whole emotional arc.

**Character description.** Sal reads as a **young adult of indeterminate gender,
late teens to early twenties in vibe** — that just-arrived, everything-is-enormous
openness of someone on their first day in a vast world. Build is slim and upright,
a little coltish, always leaning *forward* toward whatever's next. Sal is the
clearest expression of the "ledger-light person": skin the warm cream of fresh
paper, faintly engraved with neat handwritten figures and the ghost of an equals
sign, lit from **within** by a steady **terracotta-gold glow** — Sal is *born
recognized-in-potential*, radiant but unproven. Hair is a soft tousled dark-plum,
like wet ink not yet dry. Large, candid eyes the color of warm gold.

**Signature props & motifs.** Sal wears, or carries, a single keepsake: a small
**brass-and-cream tag stamped "$90,000"** on a cord around the neck — the deal's
whole value, worn like a heart locket (this is the soul that will later be *split*
into Lyle and Sunny). A faint **terracotta thread** trails from Sal's chest,
suggesting a story being spooled out. On the back of one hand, a tiny tattoo-like
mark: a downward arrow into a doorway (the dream of being *recorded*).

**Palette.** Cream skin, terracotta-gold inner glow, dark-plum ink hair and
linework, the brass `$90,000` tag in warm gold. Sal is the most saturated
terracotta presence in the cast — the accent color *is* Sal.

**Personality signifiers.** Open posture, slightly-too-eager smile, the body
language of someone who has not yet been told no. A held breath of hope.

**IMAGE PROMPT.**
> Portrait of **Sal**, a newborn transaction personified as a luminous young adult
> in their late teens, slim and upright, leaning eagerly forward as if the whole
> world just opened. Their skin is the warm cream of fresh paper, faintly engraved
> all over with neat handwritten numbers and the ghost of an equals sign, lit from
> within by a steady warm terracotta-gold glow — radiant but untested. Soft tousled
> dark-plum hair like ink not yet dry; large candid warm-gold eyes; a slightly
> too-hopeful smile. Around the neck, on a simple cord, hangs a small brass-and-cream
> tag stamped "$90,000," worn like a heart locket over the chest. A single faint
> terracotta thread trails from the chest into the air, as if a story is being
> spooled out. Simple unmarked tunic in soft cream. Three-quarter portrait, chest
> up, looking just past the viewer toward something bright off-frame. Warm cream
> background.

## LYLE — the License ($72,000, Sal's "instant" half)

**Role.** Sal's confident, quick **point-in-time** half: the **$72,000 perpetual
license**, recognized the instant the download key changes hands (control transfers
Day 1). A bit of a show-off — he arrives and is *immediately Somebody*. Recognized
fully, at once, and he knows it.

**Character description.** Lyle is the same ledger-light kind as Sal but **fully,
confidently lit** — his terracotta-gold glow is bright, even, *settled*, the glow
of value already earned and booked. Slightly older-reading than Sal: early
twenties, with the easy swagger of a person who has never had to wait for anything.
Sharper jaw, quicker eyes, a grin that's landed already. He stands in **contrapposto**,
weight cocked, chin up. Where Sal's engraving is hopeful and faint, Lyle's figures
are crisp and **finished**, stamped not handwritten — including a clean, proud
**"$72,000"** across his forearm or chest like a completed receipt.

**Signature props & motifs.** A **download key / keycard** held up between two
fingers, catching gold light — the instant of delivery, frozen at the moment of his
triumph. A short cream cape or open jacket that reads as flair. He's the "point in
time" twin, so motifs around him are **single, sharp, done**: one bright spark, one
clean stamp, a single struck match of a moment.

**Palette.** Brighter and more brass-gold than Sal; terracotta present but cooler
than Sunny-warm — Lyle leans **gold** (value realized) over terracotta. Dark-plum
crisp linework; cream garment.

**Personality signifiers.** Easy swagger, a wink, the relaxed shoulders of someone
already validated. Charismatic, a touch vain, fundamentally good-hearted — he just
got lucky in his timing and can't help knowing it.

**IMAGE PROMPT.**
> Portrait of **Lyle**, a software license personified as a confident young man in
> his early twenties, the same luminous ledger-light kind as Sal but fully and
> evenly lit — his warm terracotta-gold inner glow is bright and settled, the glow
> of value already earned and recognized. Sharper jaw, quick knowing eyes, a grin
> that has clearly already won. He stands in relaxed contrapposto, chin up, weight
> cocked, one short cream cape or open jacket suggesting flair. His skin's engraved
> figures are crisp and finished — *stamped*, not handwritten — including a clean
> proud "$72,000" across his forearm like a completed receipt. He holds a single
> glowing download keycard up between two fingers, catching gold light, as if
> frozen at the triumphant instant of delivery. A touch vain, charismatic,
> good-hearted. Three-quarter portrait, chest up, looking straight at the viewer
> with a wink-ready confidence. Warm cream background.

## SUNNY — the Support ($18,000, Sal's "patient" half)

**Role.** Sal's quiet, patient **over-time** half: the **$18,000 support
contract**, recognized **$500 a month for 36 months**. Crucially, Sunny is *born as
a liability* — **Deferred Revenue** — so she starts life *owing the world*, unearned,
and must earn her way into being *real* one month at a time. Quietly the most heroic
character in the book.

**Character description.** Sunny is the emotional counterweight to Lyle. Same
ledger-light kind, but at her introduction her glow is **cool, dim, and held back**
— she is *unearned*, deferred, waiting; her engraving reads as a debt not yet repaid.
Over the book she **warms by degrees**, terracotta light rising in her chest a little
each month, so any portrait should capture her **mid-warming**: not yet bright, no
longer dark, the most hopeful state of all. She reads warm and grounded, early
twenties, calm where Lyle is flashy. Softer features, patient eyes, a steadiness
that comes from endurance rather than ease.

**Signature props & motifs.** A small **calendar or ribbon marked in 36 segments**,
many still dim, a few near her heart glowing warm terracotta — the months already
earned. She may wear a faint **"$18,000"** that is slowly being "filled in" from
empty outline to solid gold, **$500 at a time** (some strokes complete and warm,
most still hollow). Where Lyle's motif is the single struck moment, Sunny's is the
**steady drip / the long road / the patient unwinding**. A subtle suggestion of a
**dungeon-to-doorway** behind her: she climbs out of the liability dark toward the
light.

**Palette.** Begins in cool muted plum/mauve `#6b5a6e`/`#b1a4b1` (the deferred,
unearned cool), warming toward terracotta `#b5635a` and gold `#a88a5c` at her chest.
She is the only core character who carries the **cool→warm gradient** as her whole
identity. Dark-plum ink linework throughout.

**Personality signifiers.** Quiet resolve, a small private smile, the dignified
patience of someone earning their place slowly and on purpose. Where Lyle dazzles,
Sunny *endures* — and the art should make endurance look like the braver thing.

**IMAGE PROMPT.**
> Portrait of **Sunny**, a support contract personified as a calm, grounded young
> woman in her early twenties, the same luminous ledger-light kind as Sal and Lyle —
> but where Lyle is fully lit, Sunny is caught *mid-warming*: her inner glow begins
> cool, dim muted-plum at her edges and rises to a tender warm terracotta-gold only
> at the center of her chest, as if she is slowly earning her own light. Soft
> patient features, steady hopeful eyes, a small private smile of quiet resolve. She
> holds a slender ribbon-calendar divided into 36 small segments — most still dim
> and hollow, a few near her heart filled in glowing warm terracotta, the months
> already earned. Across her forearm a faint outline reading "$18,000" is being
> filled in stroke by stroke from empty to solid gold, most of it still hollow.
> Behind her, a subtle suggestion of a dark doorway she is stepping up and out of,
> toward light. Soft cream garment. Three-quarter portrait, chest up, looking gently
> upward and forward. Warm cream background with cool-to-warm gradient echoing her
> own glow.

## REMY — the Rookie (junior accountant; the reader's avatar)

**Role.** First-week junior accountant at Summit; the reader's human stand-in.
Bewildered, earnest, takes notes, makes the mistakes a learner would make, and
learns *out loud*. Remy does the **real journal entries** — the bridge between myth
and mechanics.

**Character description.** Remy is **emphatically, ordinarily human** — the one core
character with **no inner glow** — which makes them the reader's anchor amid all the
luminous abstractions. Early-to-mid twenties, fresh out of school, a face caught
permanently between *delighted* and *overwhelmed*. Approachable and a little rumpled:
sleeves pushed up, top button undone, the look of someone who has been at the desk
since early and forgot to eat. Warm brown skin or a warm everyday complexion (keep
flexible/unstereotyped); expressive dark eyes, perpetually slightly wide. A pencil
tucked behind one ear and ink on the side of the writing hand.

**Signature props & motifs.** A **well-worn notebook** clutched or open, dense with
hand-scrawled notes, arrows, and crossed-out attempts — Remy's externalized
learning. A pencil (behind ear and/or in hand). A **mug of coffee** (Ranger Coffee,
a sly callback) going cold. A lanyard with a Summit badge. Sticky notes clinging to
a cuff. Everything about Remy says *trying hard, getting there*.

**Palette.** Grounded earth tones — soft cream shirt, gold-brown notebook, dark-plum
ink linework; terracotta only as a small warm accent (a badge, a sticky note) so
Remy sits *inside* the world's palette without competing with the glowing cast.

**Personality signifiers.** Open, anxious-but-game expression; pencil-behind-ear;
the body language of leaning in to understand. The face of "wait, *why?*" — and then
the face of "*oh.*"

**IMAGE PROMPT.**
> Portrait of **Remy**, a first-week junior accountant in their early-to-mid
> twenties — emphatically, ordinarily human with *no* magical glow, the warm anchor
> amid a luminous world. A friendly, expressive face caught between delighted and
> overwhelmed, eyes slightly wide with concentration. A little rumpled: soft cream
> shirt with sleeves pushed up and top button undone, the look of someone at the
> desk since dawn. A pencil tucked behind one ear, faint ink smudged on the side of
> the writing hand. They clutch a well-worn notebook open to a page dense with
> hand-scrawled notes, arrows, and crossed-out attempts. A Summit-badge lanyard at
> the neck; a small sticky note clinging to one cuff; a forgotten mug of coffee.
> Earthy grounded palette — cream, gold-brown, dark-plum ink — with one small
> terracotta accent on the badge. Three-quarter portrait, chest up, leaning slightly
> in toward the viewer as if trying hard to understand. Warm cream background.

## IRIS QUINN — the Controller (Remy's boss; the Socratic guide)

**Role.** Summit's controller; Remy's mentor. Sharp, kind, **Socratic** — never
hands over an answer, asks the exact question that makes Remy (and the reader) figure
it out. **Iris's questions are the interview-bank questions** in disguise.

**Character description.** Iris is **composed authority you'd want to learn from** —
forties to early fifties, the unshowy confidence of someone who has closed a hundred
books and seen every kind of mistake. Like Remy, she is **fully human, no glow**, but
where Remy is rumpled, Iris is **precise**: impeccable, deliberate, every detail
intentional. Silver threading through dark hair, worn in a clean elegant cut. A
direct, level gaze over the top of fine **gold-rimmed reading glasses** — the
signature "go on, *think*" look. The faint laugh-lines of someone who is strict but
genuinely warm.

**Signature props & motifs.** The **gold-rimmed glasses** (often pushed down, looked
over). A single **well-chosen pen** (a controller signs things). She is often shown
**mid-question** — one eyebrow up, a finger raised, a slight knowing tilt of the head
— never mid-lecture. A neat folder or a single sheet of figures held with authority.
She gestures *toward* the work, not at herself.

**Palette.** Refined and a little cooler than the rest of the cast — deep plum
`#2a1f2d` tailored clothing, a **gold** accent (glasses, pen) for her sage authority,
a terracotta scarf or detail to keep her warm and human. Crisp dark-plum linework.

**Personality signifiers.** The level over-the-glasses gaze; the raised brow of a
question already asked; calm hands. Strict but kind; the teacher who respects you
enough to make you find it yourself.

**IMAGE PROMPT.**
> Portrait of **Iris Quinn**, Summit's controller, a composed and precise woman in
> her late forties — fully human, no glow, radiating unshowy authority. Dark hair
> elegantly threaded with silver in a clean cut; fine laugh-lines; a direct, level,
> faintly amused gaze delivered over the top of gold-rimmed reading glasses pushed
> partway down her nose — the unmistakable "go on, *think*" look. She is caught
> mid-question, not mid-lecture: one eyebrow lifted, head tilted just slightly, a
> single well-chosen pen held lightly in one hand. Impeccable tailored clothing in
> deep plum with a warm terracotta scarf and small gold accents. Refined, a touch
> cooler in palette than the rest of the cast, but genuinely kind beneath the rigor.
> Three-quarter portrait, chest up, looking directly and evenly at the viewer as if
> waiting for an answer. Warm cream background.

## THE FRIAR — Luca Pacioli (the founder-sage)

**Role.** The ancient founding sage of the Accrual World: **Luca Pacioli**, the
friar-mathematician who, in the 1400s, wrote down **the Law** — *Assets =
Liabilities + Equity*. Gandalf-by-way-of-a-monastery: warm, wry, speaks in proverbs,
appears at pivotal moments. His creed: *"Every action keeps the Balance, or it does
not happen at all."*

**Character description.** A serene, timeworn **Renaissance friar**, elderly and
luminous with quiet wisdom — but rendered as a **living legend**, not a museum
portrait: he belongs to *this* warm world. A weathered, kindly face, deeply lined,
eyes bright with mischief held in check. Long silver beard. He wears the simple
**brown/cream robe of a Franciscan friar**, rope-belted. Where the transaction-people
glow with ledger-light, the Friar is haloed in **warm gold** — the sanctified value
of the realm's first principle made flesh. He stands a little outside time: utterly
calm while everything around him is in motion.

**Signature props & motifs.** An **open ledger or great book** balanced on one arm,
its two facing pages glowing faintly — **debit-left, credit-right, always equal**
(the visual of the Law). A small **set of merchant's scales** at his belt or in hand,
perfectly level. Sometimes a quill of gold. Around him, the air may carry a few
floating golden figures or an equals sign, orbiting like calm planets. A staff is
optional; the book is essential.

**Palette.** Brown-and-cream robe, **warm gold** halo and ledger-glow, dark-plum ink
linework, silver beard. The most gold-saturated figure in the cast — gold is his
signature the way terracotta is Sal's.

**Personality signifiers.** Serene half-smile; the steady, twinkling eyes of someone
who has seen every balance struck and broken and struck again; the unhurried posture
of the one who wrote the rule everyone else is still learning.

**IMAGE PROMPT.**
> Portrait of **the Friar — Luca Pacioli**, the ancient founder-sage of accounting,
> a serene Renaissance friar rendered as a living legend rather than a museum piece.
> Elderly, weathered, deeply kind face with bright mischievous eyes held in calm;
> long silver beard; the simple rope-belted brown-and-cream robe of a Franciscan
> friar. He is haloed in soft warm gold — the sanctified glow of the realm's first
> law made flesh. Balanced on one arm he holds a great open ledger whose two facing
> pages glow faintly and equally, left and right, debit and credit in perfect
> balance; at his belt hangs a small set of merchant's scales resting perfectly
> level. A few golden figures and a faint equals sign drift in the air around him
> like calm orbiting planets. Utterly serene amid motion, a knowing half-smile.
> Three-quarter portrait, chest up, looking warmly and directly at the viewer like a
> mentor about to offer a single perfect proverb. Warm cream background suffused with
> gold.

## TESS & HARI — Summit's founders (the face of Equity)

**Role.** Summit's two founders. They deposit the original **$500,000** that brings
the company into existence (**Dr Cash 500,000 / Cr Common Stock 500,000**). They
*are* **Equity** — the residual owners: last in line, biggest dreamers. Warm,
optimistic, all-in. (A two-person portrait — a pair, shown together.)

**Character description.** Tess and Hari are **the human dreamers behind the whole
machine** — a complementary founding duo, partners in build (not necessarily
romantic; read as co-founders). Both early-to-mid thirties, dressed practically for
an outdoor-tech startup: this is a company that sells climbing **gear** *and*
software, so the founders look like people who'd actually use the rope.
- **Tess** — warm, grounded, hands-on; the operator. Practical layered clothing in
  terracotta and cream, sleeves ready to work, perhaps a coil of climbing rope or a
  carabiner at her hip. Open, determined expression; the one with the plan on paper.
- **Hari** — the visionary; quick-eyed, animated, gesturing toward the horizon.
  Gold-and-plum palette, maybe a well-worn jacket. The one looking *up the mountain*.

Together they share an **easy, trusting partnership** — shoulders angled toward each
other, a between-them confidence. Faintly, around or between them, the suggestion of
**$500,000 of cash made real**: a soft warm-gold light pooled at their feet or a
glowing seed/spark they cradle together — the founding capital they are *entrusting*
to the world (credit = *credere*, to entrust).

**Signature props & motifs.** A shared founding artifact between them — a single
glowing **gold coin, seed, or spark** representing the $500,000, or a **deposit slip
/ stock certificate** held together by both. Outdoor-tech texture: a carabiner, a
coil of rope, a topo map, the Summit logo on a cap or patch. They are the *base of
the mountain* the whole book climbs.

**Palette.** Warm and optimistic — terracotta (Tess) and gold-plum (Hari), grounded
in cream, dark-plum ink linework. A pool of warm **gold** light shared between them
for the founding capital. As Equity, they should feel like the **warm foundation**
the cooler institutional characters (Iris, the Auditor) are built upon.

**Personality signifiers.** Optimism and partnership: leaning toward each other,
forward-facing, a shared "we built this" pride. Dreamers who put real money down —
brave, hopeful, last in line and first to believe.

**IMAGE PROMPT.**
> Dual portrait of **Tess and Hari**, the two co-founders of Summit, an outdoor-tech
> startup that sells climbing gear and software — warm, optimistic dreamers in their
> early thirties, shown together as trusting partners with shoulders angled toward
> each other. **Tess** (left): grounded and hands-on, the operator, in practical
> layered clothing of terracotta and cream with sleeves ready to work and a carabiner
> or coil of climbing rope at her hip, an open determined expression. **Hari**
> (right): the visionary, quick-eyed and lightly animated, in a worn jacket of gold
> and plum, gaze lifting toward a horizon. Between them they cradle a single softly
> glowing warm-gold spark — the $500,000 of founding capital they are entrusting to
> the world — its light pooling warmly at their hands. Outdoor-tech texture: a
> carabiner, a rope coil, a Summit cap or patch. They read as the warm, hopeful base
> of a mountain about to be climbed. Waist-up dual portrait, both turned slightly
> toward each other and the viewer, proud and forward-looking. Warm cream background
> with a shared pool of gold light.
