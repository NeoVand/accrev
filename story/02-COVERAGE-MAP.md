# 02 — COVERAGE MAP

*Proves the story secretly teaches the whole curriculum. Three columns of the
app's content — the **56 interview topics**, the **learn-deck parts**, and the
**deep-research backbone** — are each mapped to the chapter that is their "home."*

Two coverage promises:
1. **Every one of the 56 interview-bank topics has a home chapter** where its
   concept is taught well enough to answer the question, surfaced via *Iris's
   Questions*.
2. **Every learn-deck part is echoed** so a reader of the story reinforces the
   app's existing lessons.

## D. How coverage gets verified (consolidation)

In the consolidation phase, a **Coverage Auditor** agent reads every finished
chapter and confirms: (1) each topic's home chapter actually teaches it to
answer-readiness; (2) Iris's Questions collectively touch all 56 topics; (3) no
concept is taught twice from scratch (one home each). Any gap becomes a targeted
patch task. The per-chapter "Iris's Margin Notes" boxes are the machine-checkable
trace of which interview slugs each chapter closed.
